Healing Guide

A guide curated by the best European support players, such as Katherine Isley, Ren, and Lycrus, Kath's Healing Guide is an elaborate, beginner-friendly textbook for both new and old players, regardless of whether you lack experience or are a hardened veteran.

Teams

A section of the Healing Guide dedicated solely to team compositions - have a look at pre-existing meta comps, specific niche comps, and those for ranking modes like Origin of War. Teams range from fitting F2P to whales.

Calculator

A calculator that allows you to figure out the amount of healing, shielding, and other utility a character can provide based on their stats, equipment, and other factors. Not limited to just numberical results, the calculator also points out other strengths and niches.
